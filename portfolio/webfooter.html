Submission Form

Friend and author requested an email list submission form to be placed in the footer of his site. I was required to redesign the footer and introduce a new menu, create the copy, and deliver a solution for a one click signup. Since he was a Sci-Fi Author, I wanted to keep the theme he had started, choosing an 80’s scanline over a starfield.
